Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our talent platform

Our admin team carefully reviews each talent profile, verifying credentials, portfolio, and experience. This includes checking references and past work history to ensure high-quality talent listings.
Our platform features diverse talent categories including developers, designers, project managers, digital marketers, content creators, and more. Each talent undergoes verification to ensure expertise.
Simply browse talent profiles and click the "Hire" button on any profile. Fill out the request form with project details, timeline, and budget. The talent will receive your request and respond within 48 hours.
After registration, complete your profile with skills, experience, and portfolio. Our admin team will review your submission within 3-5 business days. Once approved, your profile becomes visible to potential clients.
We use secure payment gateways to process all transactions. Payments are held in escrow until project milestones are completed and approved by both parties, ensuring safe and transparent transactions.
